1. Introduction

Psyvrse Development Studios values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect when you interact with Psyvrse bots, how we use that data, and the measures we take to ensure your personal information remains safe. By using Psyvrse bots, you consent to the practices described herein.

2. Data Collection

To maintain, improve, and provide a high-quality experience, Psyvrse may collect the following information:

  • **Error Logs & Command Usage:** Tracks errors encountered during bot commands to identify and fix bugs.
  • **Music Interactions:** Tracks music played, playlists, or most-used tracks in music bots to enhance functionality.
  • **Global Currency & In-Game Data:** Tracks points, Chi, Pax stats, or other game-related metrics to ensure progression and fairness.
  • **Server Interaction Data:** Limited metadata about messages or reactions (no message content except for system functions like chi) to provide features and engagement tracking.

3. Data Usage

All data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve the performance, stability, and usability of Psyvrse bots.
  • Enhance features such as game progression, minigames, leaderboards, and currency systems.
  • Diagnose technical issues and ensure fair and balanced gameplay within Pax, Ludus, and Villicus.

We do not use collected data to monitor, punish, or target individual users. All metrics are aggregated or used only in context to improve the ecosystem.

4. Personal Information

Psyvrse bots do not collect private information such as:

  • Passwords
  • Email addresses
  • Payment information
  • Direct messages content outside bot interactions

No Psyvrse staff or developer will ever request this information. Any requests for personal data outside of bot functionality should be considered fraudulent.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect the integrity and security of collected data. Access to system logs, game metrics, and bot usage information is strictly limited to authorized Psyvrse developers for maintenance and improvement purposes only.

6. Rights & Control

Users have the right to:

  • Request deletion of non-essential data tracked by Psyvrse bots (e.g., Chi, Pax, Ludus progression data).
  • Opt-out of specific tracking features where possible without affecting core functionality.
  • Raise concerns or questions directly to Psyvrse support via the official Discord server.

7. Policy Changes

Psyvrse Development Studios reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated through bot announcements or the official website. Continued use of Psyvrse bots after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please contact Psyvrse Development Studios via the official Discord support server.
